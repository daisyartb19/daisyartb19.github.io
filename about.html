About Me

This is who I am!

My name is Daisy Bailey. I was born in Las Vegas, Nevada, but I moved around a lot growing up. I've lived in 7 countries on 4 continents, and this unique upbringing has given me both perspective and adaptability. My hobbie include playing rugby, training my dog, designing, and drawing. Keep reading to learn about some of my core beliefs and the experiences that made me who I am!

In terms of identity, something that is really important to me is my faith in God. My view of myself and others stems from this core belief. As C.S. Lewis wrote, "It is a serious thing to live in a society of possible gods and goddesses, to remember that the dullest most uninteresting person you can talk to may one day be a creature which,if you saw it now, you would be strongly tempted to worship". I strive to live each day remembering the divine nature of myself and others.

A core trait I believe I have is resilience. My sister will tell you that I am afraid of so many things. However, I have never once let my fear stop me from doing something I want to do. One of my favourite musicals is Newsies, in which it says,"Courage can't erase your fears. Courage is when you face your fears." I wholeheartedly believe this. It has been in some of my most fearful moments that I grew the most.
